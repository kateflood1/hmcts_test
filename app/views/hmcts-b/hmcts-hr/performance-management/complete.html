How to complete your Perfomance Management Review

  • Content owner: HMCTS HR
  • Last updated: 27 March 2016

Performance Management Reports (PMR) should capture key delivery objectives (the ‘what’), and competency-based or behavioural objectives (the ‘how’), agreed between individuals and their line managers at the start of a new performance year.

It is the individual’s responsibility to keep the report up to date and, following regular performance discussions, the report should be updated to reflect progress against agreed objectives and inform mid and end of year conversations.

In partnership with the HR Business Partner Community, a staff working group has developed a suite of products to support individuals through this process; from objective setting to completing the PMR template itself. Example PMRs for 'Development needed', 'Good' and 'Outstanding' evidence.

Products can be found under the ‘Related documents’ with a link to the approved PMR template.
