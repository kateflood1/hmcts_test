Forms

All forms are now attached and cross reference with the relevant Security and Safety Operating Procedure (SSOP), rather than being detailed in a stand alone form section. For example, the 'Record for Telephone Bomb Threats' can be found at the bottom of SSOP 12c - 'Fire and Incident Control - Bomb Threats and Suspect Packages/Letters.

The forms detailed below are mandatory requirement documents and do not form the main body of the policy.

  • HMCTS Security and Safety Delivery Plan, Quarterly Report and Risk Register
  • HMCTS Security and Safety Incident and Accident report (including near misses)
  • HMCTS Quarterly Site Security and Safety Inspection Report
  • Management of General Risk Assessments - Compliance Log
  • HMCTS Security and Safety General Risk Assessment Form - blank
  • Security and Safety Reporting and Meeting Timetable
