Training and tools

  • Content owner: HMCTS HR
  • Last updated: 27 March 2016

The aim of this learning resource is to provide line managers and staff with a range of tools to enable them to identify and meet knowledge and/ or skills gaps in using the Performance Management System effectively.

These tools, found under the ‘Related Documents’ tab, can be used by individuals, line managers, teams or peer groups to help build their knowledge, skills and capability in using the Performance Management System effectively.

A resource table showing additional ‘Learning and Guidance’ to access at key points of the Reporting Cycle throughout the performance year has also been provided.
