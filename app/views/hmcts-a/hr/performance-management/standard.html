Standard Setting and Assessing Performance - what does ‘Good’, ‘Must Improve’ and ‘Outstanding’ look like for HMCTS?

  • Content owner: HMCTS HR
  • Last updated: 27 March 2016

As we continue through the performance year, it is important that we are robust and consistent when assessing performance to encourage a high performance culture in HMCTS.

The ‘Quick Reference Guide’ on the PM Hub Homepage signposts the Civil Service ‘Performance Wave’ and ‘Performance Expectations’ which provide broad definitions for the ‘Good’, ‘Outstanding’ and ‘Development Needed’ ratings.

In partnership with the HR Business Partner Community, a staff working group has developed a series of case studies to further contextualise those definitions, and clarify the position in cases where the policy seems open or ambiguous.

The case studies can be found under the ‘Related documents’ alongside a copy of the ‘Quick Reference Guide’ and guidance on ‘Performance Assessment Expectations’.
